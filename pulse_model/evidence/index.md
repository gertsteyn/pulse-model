## Evidence Standard

An accepted evidence page should state:

- the claim being accepted
- the assumptions and boundary of the acceptance
- what is explicitly not accepted
- the written artifacts used
- the executable checks or verification commands used
- residual gaps and downstream-use rules

Durable task status remains in Beads. This section records research evidence, not task assignment.
